Opened in 2009, our team are well trained collision repair professionals, we repair thousands of vehicles for customers, we are accessible to those in the great SF Bay Area including Oakland, Alameda, Berkeley, San Leandro, San Lorenzo, and Hayward. We strive to have your vehicle back on the road as soon as possible, backed by a written lifetime warranty.

All of our work is performed with remarkable customer care, and craftsmanship, knowing we have customer satisfaction is what drives us, and we are positive you will be pleased with our results. Our staff believes in treating everyone with respect as we build customer relationships. We are here to provide any repair services your vehicle requires, and no job is too big or small for us to handle.

We are honored to serve as your auto body repair center of choice, and thank you for entrusting your vehicle to us both today and for years to come. Come in, and ask for Tac!

ALL STAR AUTO BODY is registered and licensed to perform auto body collision repairs by the California Bureau of Automotive Repair.
